Plot Theme::
Abhishek Bachchan's powerhouse performance as Gurukant Desai, complemented by Aishwarya Rai in a stellar role, maks Guru one of Hindi cinema's landmark films. Directed by Mani Ratnam, the film is loosely based on the life of one of India's most influential business tycoons. A.R. Rahman's terrific musical score, enhances the sharp screenplay and elevates the cinematic experience. The film also features Vidya Balan, R. Madhavan and Mithun Chakraborty in important roles. Released in 2007, Guru was one of the top grossing and most acclaimed films of the year.
